Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 5, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Digital Legacy App ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently.

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you create an account:

  • Email address - Required for account creation and emergency communications
  • Display name - Optional, for personalizing your experience
  • Authentication data - Encrypted password and account verification information

Emergency Contact Information

  • Contact names - Names of your designated emergency contacts
  • Contact email addresses - Email addresses for emergency notifications
  • Contact phone numbers - Phone numbers for emergency contact purposes
  • Contact addresses - Physical addresses for delivering emergency messages or materials
  • Contact relationships - Your relationship to each emergency contact

Digital Legacy Data

  • Secret notes and text - Encrypted notes and text information you store
  • File attachments - Documents, images, and other files you upload (encrypted)
  • Check-in data - Timestamps of your activity to monitor your well-being

Device and Technical Information

  • Device permissions - Storage access for file uploads
  • App usage data - Information about how you interact with the app
  • Device information - Device type, operating system version
  • Network information - Internet connectivity status for syncing data

Subscription and Billing Information

  • Subscription plan - Your chosen storage plan (FREE_50, PRO_100, PRO_200, PRO_500)
  • Billing cycle preferences - Monthly or annual billing preferences
  • Add-on selections - Additional features you've purchased

How We Use Your Information

Primary Purposes

  • Account Management - Creating and maintaining your user account
  • Emergency Services - Monitoring check-ins and sending emergency notifications
  • Data Storage - Securely storing your encrypted digital legacy information
  • Service Delivery - Providing the core functionality of the app

Emergency Processing

  • Dead Man's Switch - Automatically triggering emergency protocols when you miss check-ins
  • Emergency Notifications - Sending encrypted files to your emergency contacts
  • Message Delivery - Using emergency contact addresses to deliver physical messages or materials as specified by you
  • Temporary Decryption - Securely decrypting your files for emergency delivery (files are never stored in decrypted form)

Data Storage and Security

Encryption

  • Client-Side Encryption - All your files and secrets are encrypted on your device before upload
  • User Encryption Keys - Your personal encryption key is stored in encrypted form using your UID and our system master key
  • No Plaintext Storage - We never store unencrypted copies of your files
  • Secure Transmission - All data is transmitted using secure protocols

Storage Infrastructure

  • Firebase/Google Cloud - Data is stored on Google's Firebase platform with enterprise-grade security
  • R2 Storage - Large files are stored on Cloudflare R2 with encryption
  • Data Residency - Data is stored in secure data centers with appropriate geographic protections

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Emergency Contacts Only

  • Emergency Situations - We only share your data with your designated emergency contacts during emergency triggers
  • Automatic Processing - Emergency sharing is handled by automated systems as per your instructions
  • No Third-Party Sharing - We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes

Your Rights and Choices

Data Access and Control

  • Access Your Data - You can access all your stored information through the app
  • Modify Information - You can edit or update your information at any time
  • Download Data - You can export your data in a portable format
  • Delete Data - You can delete specific items or your entire account

Account Deletion

  • Complete Deletion - Account deletion permanently removes all your data
  • Comprehensive Cleanup - We delete data from all systems including backups
  • Emergency Contact Notification - Your emergency contacts are notified of account deletion
  • Irreversible Process - Deleted data cannot be recovered

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that your information receives adequate protection.

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts - Retained as long as your account is active
  • Emergency Data - Retained to fulfill emergency contact obligations
  • Deleted Accounts - Most data is deleted immediately upon account deletion

Security Measures

  • Encryption in Transit - All data transmission is encrypted
  • Encryption at Rest - All stored data is encrypted
  • Access Controls - Strict employee access controls
  • Regular Security Updates - We regularly update security measures

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
